## 📈 Key Insights

### Sales Performance

- Trichy generated the highest sales revenue.
- Chennai ranked second in sales performance.
- Sales peaked during June 2024.

### Product Analysis

- Laptop is the highest revenue-generating product.
- Mouse has high sales volume but lower revenue contribution.
- Printer sales remained stable throughout the period.
